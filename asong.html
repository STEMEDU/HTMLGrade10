Brown Eyed Handsome Man By Chuck Berry

"Brown Eyed Handsome Man" is a rock and roll song written and recorded by Chuck Berry,
originally released by Chess Records in September 1956 as the B-side of "Too Much Monkey Business."
It was also included on Berry's 1957 debut album, After School Session.
